Say Goodbye to WordPress

May 12, 2025 · Robert ·

I’ve spent the past two decades working in the WordPress ecosystem – building, fixing, debugging, migrating, and patching sites for clients large and small.

Here’s what I’ve learned:

Most of those sites never needed WordPress in the first place.

The average portfolio, brochure site, blog, or digital product page is better off as a static site – plain HTML, no database, no moving parts.

So I built Stattic – a Python-based static site generator built for developers, freelancers, and small teams who are done babysitting bloat.

And now I’m offering white-glove migrations for people who are finally ready to get off the WordPress rollercoaster.

Because deep down, you already know the truth:

WordPress isn’t just overkill – it’s a liability.

🧠 Who Should Migrate?

If your site falls into one of these categories, Stattic will absolutely outperform WordPress:

  • Portfolios – Designers, devs, writers, artists. Your work should speak for itself, not get lost behind a sluggish CMS.
  • Brochure Sites – Local businesses, SaaS startups, service-based teams. Clean pages, contact forms, and speed that builds trust.
  • One-Pagers – Events, launches, personal brands. Less clutter. More clarity.
  • Blogs – You write. Stattic handles the rest. Fast, SEO-optimized, easy to publish.
  • Docs Sites – Product documentation, internal wikis, API guides. Markdown-native. Built for structure.
  • Landing Pages – Run 10 variations without worrying about breaking a plugin.
  • Agencies – Build sites for clients that never break. Your future support ticket count will thank you.

If your site isn’t managing user logins, live data, or complex transactions, WordPress is dead weight.

⚙️ WordPress vs. Stattic: A Breakdown

Let’s strip it down.

Here’s the part no one tells you:

Static sites aren’t just simpler – they’re better for 90% of use cases.

And with the way Stattic handles templating, asset optimization, and SEO out of the box, you’re not trading ease for performance.

You get both.

🧭 Why I’m Doing This (and Why Now)

I didn’t build Stattic because I hate WordPress. I built it because I know WordPress too well.

I’ve watched client sites go from working to broken overnight because of one rogue plugin update.

I’ve seen performance tank just from installing a gallery plugin.

I’ve answered too many calls that started with: “Our site’s down and we don’t know why.”

WordPress used to be a revolution.

Now it’s a slow-moving bureaucracy. Bloated core, plugins on life support and a business model that traps you in constant upkeep.

Stattic is the alternative you always knew you needed.

It’s fast, secure, and designed for creators who want full control.

No lock-in, no overhead and no corporate middlemen between you and your content.

And I’m offering migrations because I know that even if you want out, the path forward can feel unclear.

So I’m making it stupid simple:

You send me your site. I send it back lighter, faster, cleaner – and 100% yours.

⚠️ Why You Can’t Afford to Wait

Look, I’m not here to scare you. But let’s be honest:

  • WordPress is a security nightmare. It’s the most attacked CMS on the web.
  • The ecosystem is fragile. One plugin update can brick your site.
  • It’s not getting better. It’s doubling down on JavaScript bloat, experimental features, and dependence on hosting companies.

And if you’re running a business or a brand?

You can’t afford downtime. You can’t afford slow pages. You can’t afford to explain to your client why the contact form stopped working – again.

WordPress isn’t free.

It’s a tax – paid in hours, stress, patches, and plugins.

Stattic? It’s freedom, up front.

✅ What You Get

When you migrate with me, here’s what’s included:

  • 🔄 Full export and rebuild of your WordPress site
  • 🧼 Clean, modern static site built with Stattic
  • 🏎 Blazing-fast HTML output, with optional Markdown content system
  • 🌍 SEO-ready pages, sitemap, and performance optimizations
  • 🛠 Help setting up hosting (GitHub Pages, Netlify, S3, Vercel, or your own)
  • ✍️ Optional content editor (Markdown GUI coming soon)

Migrations start at $2,495.

Each one is scoped specifically to your site, your goals, and your content.

This isn’t some “export plugin” slapped onto a half-baked theme. This is a custom-built, future-proof rebuild – minus the mess.

🎯 Ready to Move?

Let’s have the conversation.

If you’re done tolerating fragile updates, slow speeds, and plugin roulette – I can help you escape.

And once you’re free?

You’ll wonder why you ever put up with it in the first place.

Control is the Real Luxury

Stattic isn’t just a tool.

It’s a mindset: No one owns your content but you.

WordPress wants you updating forever.

Stattic wants you to walk away and never worry again.

This is your exit.

Take it.
