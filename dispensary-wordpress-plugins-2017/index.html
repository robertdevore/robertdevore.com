Top Dispensary WordPress Plugins

January 18, 2017 · Robert · 5 min read

Notice: This post has been updated for 2018. Within the last few months we've seen medical and recreational marijuana laws pass in 8 out of 9 states. This means there is going to be an influx of websites being built for new businesses with WordPress in the upcoming years. It doesn't matter if you're the cannabis business owner or a freelancer/agency setting up that biz owners website, you should know what WordPress plugins will help make the website you're building solid, secure and professionally focused. This article will cover the WordPress plugins that you should know about and utilize in 2017.

WordPress SEO Plugins

When it comes to search engine optimization, WordPress does a good job setting you up with a good SEO Base. But when it comes to putting all of the SEO tools needed into place, Yoast does a great job. It's the SEO plugin I use on my personal websites and the WordPress plugin I recommend to all of my clients and friends. It's the plugin I would recommend for you as well.

Yoast SEO

Additional options:

  • All In One SEO
  • SEO Wizard
  • SEO Images

WordPress Backup Plugins

If you've built websites for more than a year or two, I'm sure you've run into the moment when a website you were working on collapses into rubble and you didn't have a backup plan. Maybe it was just me 🙂 Regardless, having a back up of your website is top priority if you treat your website like a business in any type of way. With the proper backups, you can have your website go from down for the count , to back up and running in a matter of minutes. My recommendation to anyone who ever asks is UpdraftPlus. It's got everything you need in a backup solution and for most users, the free options are more than enough.

UpdraftPlus

Additional options:

  • WP-DB-Backup
  • Duplicator
  • BackupBuddy

WordPress Security Plugins

A hot topic in recent years, web security WordPress plugins needs to be a key tool in your websites toolbox. I've been a personal fan of the Sucuri plugin for my websites but have recently been testing out Wordfence and love it. It seems over a million people think so too, making it the most downloaded security plugin for WordPress.

Wordfence

Additional options:

  • Sucuri Security
  • All In One WP Security & Firewall
  • BulletProof Security

WordPress Dispensary Plugins

As the cannabis industry generates billions a year and continues going up each year, the WordPress plugins for dispensaries that cater to them and understand their industry become increasingly important.

Incoming humble brag ?

I have built WP Dispensary , the complete marijuana menu solution for WordPress and believe it is the only solution you need to power your cannabis business website. WP Dispensary gives you a complete menu solution to display each of your current products from Flowers to Concentrates, Edibles and more. There's also a growing ecosystem of add-ons that give you inventory management, WooCommerce connection and more.

WP Dispensary

Additional WP Dispensary add-ons:

  • WooConnect for WooCommerce
  • Dispensary Details for WooCommerce
  • Dispensary Inventory Management
  • Dispensary Age Verification
  • Dispensary Top Sellers
  • Dispensary Coupons
  • Dispensary Locations

WordPress Social Media Plugins

Every day we become more attached to our phones, mainly through the use of Apps, many of them being social media related. Your website is no different, and should be looking at social media as an extension of itself, interacting with users, sharing content and gaining followers and website visitors. The easiest way is to set up a WordPress plugin that adds sharing buttons to your content. MashShare is one of the top social media plugins, giving you the ability to add social media sharing buttons in a variety of ways.

MashShare

Additional options:

  • Social Media Widget by Acurax
  • Ultimate Social Media Plus
  • Social Warfare

Final notes

With the above WordPress plugins, your website will have a solid foundation to build from in 2017 and beyond! Once you've got the basics in place, you'll be able to start working on creating high quality content, making better use of twitter for marketing and build your business into a powerhouse. Then, take some time to get a high quality WordPress theme to give your website the professional visual appearance your cannabis business deserves! 2017 will be a break out year for a lot of cannabis businesses, and with the right tools and resources, one of those businesses could be yours!
