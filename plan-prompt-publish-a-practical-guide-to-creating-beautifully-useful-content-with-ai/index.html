Plan. Prompt. Publish. A Practical Guide to Creating Beautifully Useful Content with AI

May 18, 2025 · Robert ·

Most people use AI to make bad content faster. This guide flips that on its head. Over the next 9 days, I'm breaking down a complete system for using AI to create content that's actually worth reading - content that sounds like you , solves real problems, and earns attention. It's called Plan. Prompt. Publish. This isn't about shortcuts. It's about learning how to leverage AI to free up your time to do other things. I've spent the last two years building and refining this workflow to help creators, marketers, and founders write faster without sounding fake. You'll learn how to think with AI, not just write with it.

Why This Guide Exists

AI tools are everywhere – but most people use them wrong. They:

  • Let the tool dictate the voice, ending up with generic writing that sounds nothing like them
  • Prompt without purpose, leading to vague, bloated content that doesn’t serve the reader
  • Publish content they wouldn’t even stop to read themselves, let alone be proud of

That’s not efficient. That’s just noise at scale.

Done right, AI can help you:

  • Map out smarter ideas based on real audience problems, not random content calendars
  • Draft with speed and structure – giving you a solid first version you actually want to refine
  • Polish and publish without getting stuck in endless revisions or second-guessing every word
  • Build a content creation system you can use repeatedly – for blog posts, emails, social, and beyond

This guide walks you through how to make that happen, step by step.

What You’ll Learn (And When)

Each post delivers one focused chunk of the system:

PLAN (Days 1-3)

  • What makes content beautifully useful
  • The 5 questions you must answer before writing anything
  • How to use AI to research, validate ideas, and stay relevant

PROMPT (Days 4-6)

  • How to write prompts that get the tone, structure, and intent right
  • How to teach AI to match your voice
  • How to build modular prompts that save you hours per week

PUBLISH (Days 7-9)

  • How to edit AI content so it feels clean, not robotic
  • Tools and workflows for formatting, visuals, citations
  • How to build a content engine that scales with you

One post drops each day, and I’ll update this article with links to each of them so it’s all consolidated for you to come back to when needed.

Who This Guide Is For

  • Solo creators who are constantly stuck between inconsistent bursts of content and long dry spells – this will help you build a rhythm you can trust.
  • Founders wearing too many hats who know they need to publish more, but don’t have time to start from scratch every week – this framework removes friction.
  • Freelancers and consultants who want to add AI writing to their toolkit without sacrificing quality, credibility, or client tone.
  • Small teams and agencies looking to create content faster across multiple voices or brands – and do it without burning out their writers.

If you want to ship smarter, not louder – and keep your voice sharp along the way – this guide is built for you.

What You’ll Walk Away With

  • A repeatable content workflow that actually works
  • AI prompts you’ll reuse again and again
  • A faster, more effective way to write without burning out
  • Tools and examples you can plug into your own process right away

I use this system every week. You’ll be able to, too.
