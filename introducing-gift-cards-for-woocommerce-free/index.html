Introducing Gift Cards for WooCommerce® (free)

November 06, 2024 · Robert ·

Today I’m happy to announce the official release of the free Gift Cards for WooCommerce® plugin!

This plugin seamlessly integrates gift card functionality into your WooCommerce® store, letting you to enhance customer experience, boost sales, and grow brand loyalty.

The new free Gift Cards for WooCommerce® plugin is designed to provide a comprehensive solution for managing gift cards within your WooCommerce-powered online store.

Key Features

1. Admin Management Dashboard

  • Create, Edit, and Delete Gift Cards: Easily manage your gift cards from the WooCommerce® admin panel.
  • Comprehensive Gift Cards List: View all gift cards with details like code, balance, recipient email, issuance date, and expiration date.
  • Activity Logs: Monitor all actions related to gift cards, including creation, usage, balance adjustments, and deletions for better oversight and accountability.

2. Bulk Import and Export

  • CSV Import: Streamline the process of adding multiple gift cards by importing them through a CSV file.
  • CSV Export: Export all gift card data for backup or migration purposes with ease.

3. Product Integration

  • Mark Products as Gift Cards: Designate any WooCommerce® product as a gift card, allowing customers to purchase them directly.
  • Gift Card Variations: Automatically generate predefined gift card amounts (e.g., $25, $50, $100) as product variations for enhanced customer choice.

4. Customer Experience Enhancements

  • Personalized Messages: Allow customers to add personalized messages when sending gift cards.
  • Email Delivery: Option to send gift cards directly via email, ensuring timely and secure delivery.
  • My Account Integration: Customers can view and manage their active gift cards from the “My Account” section, fostering a personalized shopping experience.

5. Automated Email Notifications

  • Gift Card Delivery Emails: Schedule and send automated emails upon gift card issuance or on the specified delivery date.
  • Expiration Reminders: Configure and send reminder emails to customers before a gift card expires, encouraging them to utilize their gift cards.

Installation

Setting up Gift Cards for WooCommerce® (free) is straightforward.

Follow these steps to install and activate the plugin:

1. Download the Plugin

  • From GitHub: Visit our GitHub repository and download the latest release as a .zip file.

2. Upload and Activate

  • Upload Plugin:
    • Navigate to your WordPress dashboard.
    • Go to Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin.
    • Click Choose File and select the downloaded .zip file.
    • Click Install Now.
  • Activate Plugin:
    • Once installed, click Activate Plugin.
    • Ensure that WooCommerce is active; if not, activate WooCommerce first as this plugin depends on it.

3. Initial Setup

  • Upon activation, the plugin will create necessary database tables to store gift card data and activity logs.
  • Navigate to WooCommerce > Gift Cards to access the plugin’s admin interface.

Getting Started

Admin Dashboard

Once activated, the free Gift Cards for WooCommerce® plugin adds a new submenu under the WooCommerce® menu in your WordPress® admin dashboard.

  • Accessing Gift Cards:
    • Go to WooCommerce > Gift Cards to access the main management interface.
  • Tabs Overview:
    • Gift Cards: View and manage all existing gift cards.
    • Activity: Monitor all gift card-related activities.
    • Add Card: Issue new gift cards manually.
    • Settings: Customize email templates and set reminder periods for expiring gift cards.

Managing Gift Cards

  • Viewing Gift Cards:
    • The “Gift Cards” tab displays a list of all gift cards with details like code, balance, recipient email, issued date, and expiration date.
    • Utilize the search bar and filter options to locate specific gift cards efficiently.
  • Editing Gift Cards:
    • Click the Edit button next to a gift card to modify its details.
    • A modal window will appear where you can update the balance, expiration date, recipient email, sender name, and personal message.
    • Upon successful submission, a success message will display within the modal.
  • Deleting Gift Cards:
    • Click the Delete button next to a gift card to remove it.
    • A confirmation prompt ensures that you intend to delete the gift card, preventing accidental removals.

Importing and Exporting Gift Cards

  • Exporting Gift Cards:
    • Navigate to the “Gift Cards” tab.
    • Click on the Export CSV button to download a CSV file containing all gift card data.
  • Importing Gift Cards:
    • Click on the Import CSV button.
    • Select a valid CSV file containing gift card data.
    • The plugin processes the file and imports the gift cards in batches to ensure optimal performance.
    • Upon completion, a success or error message will display based on the import results.

Integrating Gift Cards with Products

  • Marking Products as Gift Cards:
    • Edit a product in WooCommerce.
    • In the “Product Data” section, check the Gift Card option to designate the product as a gift card.
  • Gift Card Fields on Product Pages:
    • On the frontend, gift card products display additional fields for:
      • Gift Card Type: Digital or Physical.
      • Recipient Email: Email address of the gift card recipient.
      • Sender Name: Name of the sender.
      • Message: Personalized message for the recipient.
      • Delivery Date: Date when the gift card should be delivered.

Customer Experience

  • Purchasing Gift Cards:
    • Customers can add gift card products to their cart and proceed to checkout.
    • During checkout, they can apply gift card codes to avail discounts based on their gift card balances.
  • Managing Gift Cards:
    • After logging into their account, customers can navigate to the Gift Cards section under “My Account.”
    • Here, they can view all active gift cards, their balances, and expiration dates.

Future Enhancements

While Gift Cards for WooCommerce® version 1.0.0 is packed with essential features, I am committed to improving this plugin further.

Future updates will likely include:

  • Enhanced Reporting: Advanced analytics and reporting tools for better insights into gift card usage and performance.
  • Additional Customization Options: More flexibility in customizing gift card templates and settings.
  • Integration with Other Plugins: Compatibility with popular WooCommerce® extensions for expanded functionality.
